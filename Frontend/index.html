Our Mission

Green Scouts empowers youth and communities to take meaningful climate action through tree planting initiatives, environmental education programs, and advocacy work. We combine traditional scouting values with modern environmental stewardship to create the eco-leaders of tomorrow.

Tree Planting

Organizing community tree planting events to restore ecosystems and combat climate change.

Environmental Education

Teaching youth about ecology, conservation, and sustainable practices through hands-on learning.

Scouting Programs

Developing leadership and outdoor skills while fostering environmental responsibility.

LYDIA ONYANGO

CEO and The Founder

Lydia Onyango is a Biochemistry major from the University of Nairobi, with a strong foundation in microbiology, biotechnology, quality assurance, and biomedical research. She's also a dynamic and visionary leader, an active Rover Scout, and her journey is defined by an unyielding passion for environmental conservation, human health, leadership, and governance. This powerful synthesis drives her impactful contributions. Throughout her university tenure, Lydia distinguished herself as a prominent student leader, serving in various capacities across diverse organizations. Her commitment to fostering positive change extended beyond campus, where she also dedicated her time and energy as a youth leader in several community-based organizations. These experiences honed her exceptional leadership acumen, strategic thinking, and ability to mobilize and inspire. An ardent environmental enthusiast, Lydia's deep-seated concern for the planet's well-being and its intrinsic link to human health propelled her to co-found GROVE ‚Äì Green Remediation of Viable Ecosystems. This groundbreaking initiative is dedicated to tackling the critical challenges of chemical waste and heavy metal pollution. As a driving force behind GROVE, Lydia is instrumental in developing innovative solutions and advocating for sustainable practices that safeguard our ecosystems and promote a healthier future for all. Lydia's unique blend of scientific understanding, leadership experience, and unwavering dedication to environmental stewardship positions her as a formidable advocate for change. Her work with GROVE exemplifies her commitment to translating passion into tangible action, striving to create a world where human well-being and ecological vitality are inextricably linked and thriving. Her journey is a testament to the power of youth-led initiatives in addressing some of the most pressing global challenges. +
